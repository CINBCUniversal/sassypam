Whitespace

Letter Description
p,m padding, margin
a,t,r,b,l all, top, right, bottom, left
n,s,m,l none (0px), small (5px), medium (10px), large(20px)

Mix and match these to get your desired effect. You can put them on any element.

I have 0 padding
I have lots of padding!
I have 0 margin
I have lots of margin!
<div class='content-border pan'>I have 0 padding</div>
<div class='content-border pal'>I have lots of padding!</div>
<div class='content-border man'>I have 0 margin</div>
<div class='content-border mal'>I have lots of margin!</div>
